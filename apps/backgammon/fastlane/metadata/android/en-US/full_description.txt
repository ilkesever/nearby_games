Nearby Backgammon lets you challenge friends and players around you to a classic game of backgammon over Bluetooth — no internet required.

Simply open the app, discover nearby players, and start a match in seconds. Roll the dice, move your checkers, and enjoy the timeless strategy of backgammon face-to-face with anyone around you.

Features:
• Play backgammon with nearby players via Bluetooth
• No internet connection required
• Classic backgammon rules with a beautiful board
• Pass & Play mode for two players on one device
• Fast and easy matchmaking
• Works anywhere — cafes, parks, travel
