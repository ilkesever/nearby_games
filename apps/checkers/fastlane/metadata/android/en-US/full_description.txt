Nearby Checkers lets you challenge friends and players around you to a classic game of checkers (international 10×10 draughts) over Bluetooth — no internet required.

Simply open the app, discover nearby players, and start a match in seconds. Move your pieces, jump your opponent's, and enjoy the timeless strategy of checkers face-to-face with anyone around you.

Features:
• Play checkers with nearby players via Bluetooth
• No internet connection required
• International draughts rules with a beautiful 10×10 board
• Pass & Play mode for two players on one device
• Fast and easy matchmaking
• Works anywhere — cafes, parks, travel
