Privacy Policy

Nearby Chess & Nearby Backgammon  ·  Effective date: March 7, 2026  ·  Last updated: April 6, 2026

Nearby Games apps (Nearby Chess, Nearby Backgammon) do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. All gameplay is strictly peer-to-peer over Bluetooth between nearby devices. No internet connection is required or used.

1. Overview

Nearby Games apps ("the Apps") — Nearby Chess and Nearby Backgammon — connect devices locally using Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) for two-player gameplay. There are no user accounts, no servers, no analytics, and no data of any kind is sent off your device.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personal information, including but not limited to:

  • Name, email address, or any account information
  • Location data
  • Device identifiers
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Crash reports
  • Game history or scores

3. Bluetooth Usage

The App requests access to Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) solely to discover and connect with another nearby device running the same App. Bluetooth is used exclusively for:

  • Advertising the App's presence to other nearby players
  • Scanning for other nearby players
  • Exchanging game moves directly between two devices

No Bluetooth data is stored beyond the active game session. No data is forwarded to any third party or external server. The Bluetooth connection is terminated when the game ends or the App is closed.

4. Data Storage

The App does not persist any data between sessions. No files are written to your device beyond what the operating system requires to run the application (e.g., app binary and assets).

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, advertising networks, or cloud services. There are no in-app purchases and no network requests of any kind.

6. Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal data from any user, including children under the age of 13. It is safe for use by children.

7. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy is updated, the new version will be posted at this URL with a revised "Last updated" date. Since no personal data is collected, any changes are unlikely to affect your privacy.
