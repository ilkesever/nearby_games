Nearby Chess lets you challenge friends and players around you to a classic game of chess over Bluetooth — no internet required.

Simply open the app, discover nearby players, and start a match in seconds. Enjoy the timeless strategy of chess face-to-face with anyone around you.

Features:
• Play chess with nearby players via Bluetooth
• No internet connection required
• Classic chess rules with a beautiful board
• Fast and easy matchmaking
• Works anywhere — cafes, parks, travel
