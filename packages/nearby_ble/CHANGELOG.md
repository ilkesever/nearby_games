## 0.1.0

* Initial release: cross-platform BLE plugin for peer-to-peer multiplayer games.
* Supports iOS (CoreBluetooth) and Android BLE APIs.
* Central and Peripheral roles — discover, connect, and exchange JSON messages without internet or WiFi.
* Handles Android 12+ permission model (`BLUETOOTH_SCAN`, `BLUETOOTH_ADVERTISE`, `BLUETOOTH_CONNECT`).
